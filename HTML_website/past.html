★ MY PAST。★

Visual Arts was one of the subjects I studied during year 11 and 12

It was one of my favourite subjects, as I enjoyed analysing artists and their paintings. Theodore Gericault was one of the artists I studied, and he painted "The Raft of the Medusa" on the year 1819. I also studied the artist Francisco De Goya, who painted "The Third of May 1808" on the year 1814.

I participated in volunteer events my school provided

It was just some simple fund raising events and tidying up the classrooms. It gave me opportunities to get involved with my school community, which I really enjoyed as my friends attended these volunteer events with me.

During lunch at school, I sometimes play chess with one of my friends

I improved after every match, although I stopped playing much after I graduated from highschool. I mostly lost to my friend, however I still had fun playing chess with her. I surprisingly found it enjoyable and a competetive game.
